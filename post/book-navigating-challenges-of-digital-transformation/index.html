📕 Navigating the Challenges of Digital Transformation | Available Now

July 6, 2025 - 3 minutes read - 569 words

The third industrial revolution gave birth to the digital web—a new era of global connectivity and digital intelligence. My book Exploring the Metaverse, covers it and explore what comes next. As we now step into an AI-driven world, we must ask: where are we truly heading, and how ready are we?

Transformation requires us to evolve—from traditional methods to newer, more adaptive approaches. While the digital era has been around for decades, many industries still struggle with achieving meaningful digital transformation. Change is constant—but not easy.

At Thoughtworks, we pioneered Agile software development and continue to evolve its application through practices like Async-Agile. In today’s AI first landscape, it is even more critical to address the core challenges of transformation—not just optimizing processes, but adopting a holistic approach. True digital transformation cannot happen in silos.

📖 Navigating the Challenges of Digital Transformation

This is exactly where Diljeet Saluja’s new book, ‘Navigating the Challenges of Digital Transformation’, becomes essential reading. Diljeet, a long-time champion of delivery excellence, presents a thoughtful and practical framework for improving operational efficiency and driving sustainable transformation.

My Connection with the book!

“Your ‘Championing Fixed Bid’ is a great series.
I see a book in the making… If you haven’t thought of it yet, you should.” — Sep 5, 2023, 10:45 AM

I sent this message to Diljeet after getting impressed from his interactive workshop series “Championing Fixed Bid”. That casual comment has now become a full-fledged book—and I was honored to have this moment quoted in the book’s acknowledgments and at the launch event. It was truly special to exchange signed copies of our books.

I had the privilege of getting early access to the book, contributing a testimonial, and attending the book launch event. 🙏 A heartfelt thank you to Diljeet for including my thoughts alongside those of other esteemed leaders.

📖 My testimonial

Diljeet’s book is a timely and essential guide to digital transformation in the AI era. As industries strive to keep pace with rapid advancements, this book bridges the gap between -driven transformation and the challenges organizations face in adapting to change.

Drawing from over 30 years of experience in digital transformation—spanning agile development to AI-first software development—Diljeet offers deep industry insights into how organizations and their partners can mature and lead the future of digital transformation. The book delves into critical aspects such as commercial and revenue management, the complexities of large-scale transformations, and the importance of aligning efforts with business value.

In an era where the only constant is change, agility is crucial. This book explores the constraints and challenges of managing transformation while maintaining agility. More importantly, it highlights the key traits that stakeholders and organizations must develop to successfully navigate this evolving landscape.

Through real-world experiences and firsthand accounts from IT professionals, Diljeet provides invaluable lessons on the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in an AI-driven world. This book not only prepares readers for what’s coming but also illustrates how AI can be leveraged to drive meaningful and sustainable transformation.

Kuldeep Singh,
Principal Consultant, Emerging Technologies, Thoughtworks
Author - “Exploring the Metaverse”
